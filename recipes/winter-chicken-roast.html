One-Pan Winter Chicken Roast

A one-pan roast chicken with winter veggies.

This is a super easy dish to make. Simply cut your veggies, place on a pan with your chicken, season, bake and enjoy!

Recipe by Tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 large sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 lb brussels sprouts, halved
  • 2 apples, sliced (wedged)
  • 4 skin on chicken thighs
  • 4 Tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Tablespoon fresh rosemary
  • 2 Tablespoons fresh thyme

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F, 200°C.
  2. On a baking sheet, toss the sweet potato, Brussels sprouts, and sliced apples so theyâ€™re evenly distributed.
  3. Lay the chicken thighs on top.
  4. Drizzle the olive oil over the chicken, fruit, and veggies, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Sprinkle with the garlic, rosemary, and thyme.
  6. Turn the chicken over and season on the other side, then return to skin-side up.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes, then broil for 5 - 10 minutes so the chicken skin gets golden brown and crispy.
  8. Enjoy!
