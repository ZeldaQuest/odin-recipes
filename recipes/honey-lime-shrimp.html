Honey Lime Shrimp

A low calorie option for shrimp, asparagus and peppers.

This dish provides an excellent amount of protein and fiber at just under 300 calories a serving. Quick and simple to make, this is a great lunch to perk up your day!

Recipe by Tasty.

Ingredients

  • Oil, to taste
  • 1 lb raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 2 teaspoons garlic, minced, separate in half
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 lb asparagus, cut in half
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add shrimp, salt, pepper, 1 teaspoon of garlic, honey and lime juice. Mix well. Cook for 2 minutes, or until bottom of shrimp turns pink and opaque.
  3. Flip the shrimp and cook an additional 2 minutes or until shrimp are fully pink and there are no gray bits. Remove shrimp from skillet and set aside.
  4. In a fresh skillet (or you can clean out the same one), add oil and saut√© the asparagus and bell pepper with salt, pepper, and the remaining garlic.
  5. When the asparagus and bell pepper have reached your desired doneness, add the shrimp back into the skillet and combine.
  6. Serve immediately.
  7. Enjoy!
